About Me


Born and raised in Baguio City, Philippines.


I love creating art; it's definitely my biggest passion in life. Grew up seeing the world in different colors, used it to escape reality and merge with art instead. I love looking at things in a different perspective, it helps with gaining inspiration and turning the most mundane thing in the world into something special.


Interests


I like to dive deep into the unknown—things that people usually find uneasy—and try to emulate the feeling that provokes the inner psyche. It's an occuring theme within my works. I think a lot of my inspirations come from a lot of Horror films/games, Indie films, documentaries, or just a lot of niche works. I'm a huge fan of things that depicts anything Horror, or something that has to do with the psychology of the human mind.
